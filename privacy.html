Privacy Policy

Effective: April 1, 2026

Split is a daily opinion game built by Cortex Studios. We believe your data is yours. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

The short version: we collect very little. No email. No phone number. No location. Your picks stay on your device. We use anonymous analytics to improve the app.

Information We Collect

When you use Split, we collect only what you directly provide during setup: a first name and a username. Both are chosen by you and can be anything you want. We do not require your real name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information to use the app.

Your picks, stats, and streaks are stored locally on your device. Pick data is also sent anonymously to our servers to calculate crowd results (the percentage split you see after picking).

If you post a comment on a question, your chosen display name and comment text are stored on our servers so other users can see them. Comments are public within the app.

Analytics

Split uses PostHog, a product analytics service, to understand how people use the app. PostHog collects anonymous usage data such as which screens you visit, which questions you pick, and how long you spend in the app. This data is tied to an anonymous identifier, not your real name or any personal information.

PostHog may collect device information (device model, operating system version) for the purpose of ensuring app compatibility. PostHog's privacy policy is available at posthog.com/privacy.

You can request deletion of your anonymous analytics data by contacting us at the address below.

How We Use Your Information

Your username and first name are used to display your identity alongside your comments and in your profile. Anonymous analytics data is used solely to improve the app experience — understanding which questions resonate, where users get confused, and what features to build next. We do not use any information for advertising or profiling.

Daily Questions

The daily question content is fetched from our servers (hosted by Supabase) when the app opens. This is a one-way data transfer: the server sends the questions to your device. Your device does not send any personal information to our servers during this process.

Third-Party Services

Split uses the following third-party services:

Supabase provides our question database, comments storage, and anonymous user records. When the app fetches questions or posts a comment, it connects to Supabase servers. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

PostHog provides anonymous product analytics. Usage events are sent to PostHog's servers for analysis. No personally identifiable information is shared with PostHog. PostHog's privacy policy is available at posthog.com/privacy.

Apple provides the App Store through which you download Split. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's own privacy policy at apple.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, social media SDKs, or third-party tracking for ad targeting.

Data Storage and Security

Your personal data (name, username, picks, stats) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage provided by your operating system. Comments you post are stored on our servers (Supabase) with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

We do not maintain traditional user accounts with passwords. There is no cloud backup of your personal pick history. If you delete the app, your local data is permanently removed from your device. Comments you posted will remain visible to other users unless you request their deletion.

Children's Privacy

Split is designed for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond a self-chosen first name and username. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, photos, or any other sensitive information from any user, including children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through Split that concerns you, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (such as "72% of users picked YES on this question") may be displayed within the app as part of the core experience. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or subpoena. We would notify affected users unless legally prohibited from doing so.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Since most of your data is stored locally on your device, you have direct control over it. To reset your local data, you can delete and reinstall the app.

To request deletion of your comments or anonymous analytics data from our servers, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves. If we make material changes, such as introducing user accounts or new data collection, we will notify you through the app before the changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
